How to import:
 -> "https://cstimer.net"
 -> "Export" (second icon in upper left corner)
 -> "Export/Import only Options"
 -> Paste the "STYLE_DATA_STRING" into the pop-up and press "OK"
 -> enjoy the very cool styled timer ;)
